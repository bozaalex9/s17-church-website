# S17 Page Experiences

## Shared story arc

Every page should move visitors from orientation toward relationship and action:

1. Proclaim the page's central truth.
2. Establish human context through witness and story.
3. Explain what participation means.
4. Offer one clear next step.

## Homepage

The homepage is the signature proclamation: documentary hero, welcome, Gather, Grow, Go, and final invitation. It should carry the site's greatest cinematic energy and clearest sense of joyful family.

## Interior pages

- **Gather:** worship, presence, welcome, and shared rhythm may feel expansive and celebratory.
- **Grow:** formation should feel alive, relational, and directional—not academic or quiet by default.
- **Go:** mission should feel urgent, outward, hopeful, and grounded in real witness.
- **About:** story may be expressive; beliefs and doctrine remain clear, stable, and reverent.
- **Visit:** confidence and warmth lead; logistics remain immediate and calm.
- **Give:** generosity may be joyful, but trust, security, and comprehension govern interaction.

No page should manufacture urgency, hide practical details, or use energy to pressure a decision.
