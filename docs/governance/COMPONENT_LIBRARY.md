# S17 Component Library Guidance

## Purpose

Components are reusable instruments of proclamation. They should encode hierarchy, accessibility, responsive behavior, and motion meaning—not flatten every page into identical blocks.

## Component character

- Heroes support decisive headlines, documentary media, layered depth, and clear handoff.
- Editorial sections give one idea visual authority and sequence detail after comprehension.
- Story and pathway cards may use expressive entrance and depth when their content benefits from discovery.
- CTAs arrive after orientation and state one clear action.
- Navigation remains predictable, mathematically balanced, keyboard accessible, and visually confident.
- Practical cards, forms, doctrine, giving, and care components remain calmer and information-first.

## Variants

Prefer meaningful variants such as `sacred`, `editorial`, `cinematic`, `practical`, or `high-energy` over arbitrary visual knobs. Defaults must be accessible. Expressive variants must include reduced-motion and mobile behavior.

## Proclamation revision

Reusability must not force uniform restraint. Shared primitives should provide controlled ranges of energy so pages can proclaim according to meaning without one-off code.
